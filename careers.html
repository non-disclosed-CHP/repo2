PMI Training Overview

PMI Training offers courses that are best suited for your training needs, both in-person and virtual. PMI’s core offerings have always been dedicated to your professional development in project management and building your PMI Talent Triangle® – a combination of expertise in Ways of Working, Power Skills, and Business Acumen, as well as digital skills. PMI Training continues to enhance its learning portfolio to address the needs of our ever-changing environment.

Why Should You Attend PMI Training?

Whether you’re looking to build your leadership expertise, gain valuable skills to drive project success, or learn the basics of agile, it’s time to immerse yourself in our dynamic instructor-led training. These trainings are specifically developed for project managers by project management experts. PMI Training provides a highly interactive small-group experience in two formats: in-person and virtual. Choose from more than 100 trainings across a variety of subject areas that range in duration from 1-hour to 1-4 days, instructed in English (unless otherwise indicated).

Search through the list of upcoming opportunities in the PMI Training Catalog. Check back often as they continue to expand their programming.
