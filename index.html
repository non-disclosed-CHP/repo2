Welcome to AMU Student Veterans of America

Empowering Veterans through Community, Resources, and Support. At AMU SVA, we believe in the power of community and the strength that comes from helping each other. As student veterans, we understand the unique challenges that come with balancing academics, transitioning from military life, and building a career.

AMU Student Veterans of America Chapter

A supportive, student-led, family-centered community bridging the gap between service and academic success. Our chapter connects military veterans with valuable resources and networks to help them succeed in their educational and professional endeavors. Whether you’re navigating the GI Bill, seeking career advice, or looking for a place to connect with like-minded individuals, AMU SVA is here to help. Our community extends beyond the classroom; we foster lasting bonds through social events, career fairs, workshops, and other initiatives designed to help veterans feel at home at AMU. We work closely with university administration and veteran organizations to address the specific challenges and opportunities of military life transition, whether through scholarship resources, exclusive internship programs, or family support events, ensuring a smooth journey for each veteran and their loved ones.
