Welcome to AMU Student Veterans of America

Empowering Veterans through Community, Resources, and Support. At AMU SVA, we believe in the power of community and the strength that comes from helping each other. As student veterans, we understand the unique challenges that come with balancing academics, transitioning from military life, and building a career. That’s why we are committed to providing the resources and support you need to thrive academically and professionally, while building lasting relationships with fellow veterans.

AMU Student Veterans of America Chapter

A supportive, student-led, family-centered community bridging the gap between service and academic success. Our chapter connects military veterans with valuable resources and networks to help them succeed in their educational and professional endeavors. Whether you’re navigating the GI Bill, seeking career advice, or looking for a place to connect with like-minded individuals, AMU SVA is here to help. Our community extends beyond the classroom; we foster lasting bonds through social events, career fairs, workshops, and other initiatives designed to help veterans feel at home at AMU.

Who We Are

Our leadership team, composed of veterans and advocates, is dedicated to guiding members through their academic and professional paths. We are committed to fostering a community of support, advocacy, and empowerment for all student veterans at AMU/APU. We provide mentorship, resources for academic success, and networking opportunities with professionals in various industries. Additionally, we actively work to ensure that the voices of student veterans are heard within the university administration and beyond. Whether it’s advocating for mental health services, hosting career development workshops, or providing guidance on utilizing benefits, our team is here to make sure you’re equipped with everything you need to succeed.
