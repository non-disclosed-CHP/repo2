AMU SVA News

Date: 11/4/2023

We are proud to announce that our SVA Chapter has made a meaningful contribution of $100 to Operation Underground Railroad (O.U.R.)!

At the end of 2023, we are committed to making a positive impact, and O.U.R.’s mission to combat human trafficking resonates with us deeply. Every dollar counts, and together, we can help fight against this heinous crime.

Join us in supporting O.U.R. and their incredible work to rescue and rehabilitate survivors. Let’s make a difference together!
